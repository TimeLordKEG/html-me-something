Welcome to The Temple of SharkJesus

We are a puntastic religion which worships the divine Megalodon. We also honor your inner Megalodon.

SharkJesus gives us the gills with which to swim the seas of life. All hail His Biteyness.

By your gills, may you be freed, to shake your flippers just the way you want to be!

The Temple of SharkJesus is a sound camp at regional Burning Man events
for both Midwest Burners & Gateway Burners.

We provide much more than multiple genres of music to dance and worship with.
We also offer flesh marinades - a fragrant and refreshing mud bath that nourishes your skin.
If you feel inspired by SharkJesus and want to forever live in his watery graces, his love will "swallow" you whole in baptisim.
There are minions to be found all throughout the burns working to bring "soles" into to SharkJesus' loving bite.

These events are focused on the ten principles of Burning Man with the addition of an 11th principle - Consent.

The Message of the Acolybites

As the blood and carnage dragged on, SharkJesus dove deep under the sea. He communed with the urchins, seaweed, and the reefer.
On the third day, he arose from the surf and decided that all their love should be shared with human-kind.

Even though SharkJesus loved us all, many humans were tricked by the deception of the devious serpent SharkSatan.
They forced SharkJesus to walk into the desert while dragging an anchor wearing nothing more than a crown of six-pack rings.
Even after human-kinds betrayal, SharkJesus loved us. He promised that his love will swallow us whole.
And we should not shun the nonbelievers, for they populate the beaches and feed Him!
